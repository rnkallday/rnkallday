About

Writer, web builder, and lifelong learner based in Seattle.

The Short Version

I'm a creative generalist who loves the intersection of words, design, and technology. Whether I'm crafting exhibition websites for world-class museums, writing personal essays, or nerding out over vinyl records, I bring curiosity and craft to everything I do.

Currently, I build digital experiences for cultural institutions like the Seattle Art Museum and MOHAI. My writing has appeared in The Sun Magazine, HuffPost, MakeUseOf, and other publications.

What I Do

My work lives at the crossroads of content and code. I believe great digital experiences start with great storytelling — and that the best websites feel less like brochures and more like conversations.

Web Development Content Strategy UX Writing Personal Essays Tech Writing Brand Voice HTML/CSS Journalism

When I'm Not Working

You'll find me digging through crates at the record store, experimenting with music production, or exploring the Pacific Northwest with a camera in hand. I'm endlessly fascinated by how things work — whether that's a vintage synthesizer, a well-designed interface, or a perfectly constructed sentence.
